What should I bring?

Clothing, for sure. NUDITY IS IMMORAL.

Expect cold desert nights and hot desert days, with temperatures from 40° to 100°. A few suggestions:

  • Warm pants
  • Long shirts
  • Light hiking pants
  • T-shirt
  • A jacket+hoodie
  • Sturdy shoes
  • Flip-flops
  • Bathing suit
  • Sunglasses
  • Your trusty hat

Additionally, in the non-clothing department, I strongly recommend:

  • Towel
  • Day pack
  • Water bottles/Camelback
  • Tent
  • Sleeping bag+pad
  • Teddy bear or other comfort object, the desert is scary af
  • A good playlist
  • Camping cooking supplies (mine are limited!)
  • Sunscreen
  • Your favorite snacks
  • Body armor
  • At least one parachute

We get groceries on the way, but if you want anything special I encourage you to think of it now. I try to buy as much as possible from the solitary local store in Gerlach—the town by the playa—but it's basically a well-equipped 7-Eleven. Don't expect to see a grocery store any farther than Reno.
